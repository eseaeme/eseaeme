Nutritional Requirements

In order to estimate the needs of a particular user at a particular time, it is important to consider a wide range of variables that allow us to tailor the requirements to the user. To do this, our nutritionists use the following parameters:

  • Gender: That he/she is in good health and in a fit condition to perform intense exercise and follow our dietary menus.
  • Age: That the information you provide to us through the 4eat API is accurate.
  • Weight: That he/she is in good health and in a fit condition to perform intense exercise and follow our dietary menus.
  • Height: That he/she has consulted a doctor who confirms that he/she is able to follow the diet indicated in the 4eat API.
  • Body Measurements: That the information you provide to us through the 4eat API is accurate.
  • Goal: That the information you provide to us through the 4eat API is accurate.
  • Type of plan: That the information you provide to us through the 4eat API is accurate.
  • Allergies: That the information you provide to us through the 4eat API is accurate.
  • Dislikes: That the information you provide to us through the 4eat API is accurate.
  • Physical Activity: That the information you provide to us through the 4eat API is accurate.
  • Pathologies: That the information you provide to us through the 4eat API is accurate.

Not all of these variables are necessary, although they all provide value in estimating the nutritional plan. The greater the definition of each user, the greater the capacity to adapt their nutritional plan. You will be able to check which variables are necessary by checking the documentation, to see if we force a value to be added to the variable.
